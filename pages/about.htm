About

Hi, I'm Chris. I'm an Interactive Developer working from Brighton, UK. Below is a little bit about me

Skills

Through new and exciting projects I have developed my skills across a variety of languages and platforms such as JavaScript, C# and ActionScript. I grasp every opportunity to proactively learn and use new technologies. This self motivation and drive has lead me to progress from once a Graphic Designer to an Interactive Developer.

My past Graphic design experience has allowed me to look at user experience and development approach in new ways.

Over the past few years I have used the following frameworks: PixiJS, ThreeJS, Phaser, CreateJS, BackboneJS, RequireJS and many others. I also have experience with Flash, Adobe Air, Unity and Apache Cordova.

Experience

I graduated from the Arts University Bournemouth (AUB) with a degree in Graphic Design in 2007. Since then I have worked at a number of different agencies in London such as PlayerThree and DigitasLBI. Before moving to Brighton where I was the Senior Developer at Kanoti. Unfortunatly Kanoti closed but it allowed me to create a freelance career in Development. I've been working with high profile clients such as BBC, Channel4, Disney and more. I understand the importance and love to create the highest quality work. I do this by working closely with teams of designers, produces and clients.
